Our Story

Founded in 2024, ShriekedIn emerged from a conversation between Halloween enthusiasts who realized there was no centralized platform for the Halloween community. While there were scattered Facebook groups, seasonal pop-up websites, and local event listings, nothing brought it all together in a professional, year-round platform.

We started by talking to haunted attraction owners, costume shop proprietors, event organizers, and Halloween superfans. The message was clear: the Halloween industry needed a dedicated professional network. A place where businesses could promote their services, organizers could find vendors, and enthusiasts could discover experiences.

Today, ShriekedIn serves thousands of users across the country, from major metropolitan areas to small towns with rich paranormal histories. We're constantly growing and improving, adding new features based on community feedback.
