Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. During peak Halloween season (September-October), responses may take slightly longer.

Can I report inappropriate content?

Yes! Please use this contact form to report any content that violates our community guidelines. Include the URL of the content and a brief description of the issue.

How do I become a verified business?

Business verification is available for all business account holders. Send us a message through this form with "Business Verification" in the subject line, and we'll guide you through the process.

I have a partnership opportunity. Who should I contact?

We love hearing about partnership opportunities! Please use this contact form with "Partnership Inquiry" in the subject line, and our business development team will reach out to you.

What if I found a bug or technical issue?

Thank you for helping us improve! Please report any bugs or technical issues through this form with "Bug Report" in the subject line. Include details about what happened, what page you were on, and your browser/device information if possible.
