2. Legal Basis for Processing

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your spectral signature (personal data) to:

  • Provide Services: Create and maintain your account, display your content, facilitate connections
  • Improve Platform: Analyse usage patterns, develop new features, fix bugs
  • Communicate: Send account notifications, updates, Halloween event alerts (with your consent)
  • Security: Prevent fraud, protect against unauthorised access, ensure platform safety
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations, respond to legal requests
  • Marketing: Send promotional emails (only with your explicit consent - you can unsubscribe anytime)

5. How We Share Your Data

We never sell your personal data. We share data only in these specific circumstances:

Information you choose to make public (profile, posts, reviews) is visible to other users and potentially search engines. Choose wisely what you reveal from beyond the veil!

5.2 Service Providers

We share data with trusted partners who help us operate:

  • Hosting: Heroku (servers in EU/UK region)
  • Email: For account and notification emails
  • Analytics: Google Analytics (anonymised data)
  • CDN: For content delivery

All service providers are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only as instructed.

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or to protect rights, property, or safety.

5.4 Business Transfers

If ShriekedIn is acquired or merged, your data may be transferred. You'll be notified of any change in ownership or data practices.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations:
